About Care.com Care.com is a comprehensive self-service solution for your family care needs. The online platform helps you connect with caregivers in your area by searching the caregiver network or posting a caregiver job and screening applicants. The network includes: Child care providers (nannies, one-time sitters, care shares, day cares, etc.) Tutors Senior caregivers Pet caregivers Housekeepers Errand helpers Care.com provides additional resources to support your experience: Visit the Safety Center for the Care.com safety pledge and tips and tools to help you have safe experiences. Explore a library of articles and guides on a variety of topics. About Your Premium Membership The University of Oregon pays for your monthly membership so you can: Search caregiver profiles on Care.com and limit the results to specific requirements. Post jobs with your care needs and requirements so interested caregivers can apply. Use site tools and resources such as hiring guides and rate calculators. The premium membership also allows you to: Contact caregivers directly to schedule interviews. View and reply to applications and other messages using your Care.com account. Purchase background checks. Access LifeMart discount program . Access Care Talks , monthly webinars and events. Use a discount on HomePay tax services. Enroll in Care.com To access your Care.com membership, you will need to register an account: Go to https://uoregon.care.com . Provide required information - first name, last name, date of birth, street address, and zip code. Confirm eligibility with your DuckID. Your Duck ID is the first part of your UO email address before the @ sign. Example: daisy@uoregon.edu , daisy is the DuckID. Answer a few questions about your caregiving needs. Start your search. Enroll in Care.com Accommodations will be provided to any individual who is unable to fully interact with the Care.com website. Employees can contact Human Resources to request assistance and/or accommodations by emailing hrinfo@uoregon.edu or by calling 541-346-3159. Appointments can be made to come to the HR office and meet with an HR representative during normal business hours. Did your eligibility check fail? First, make sure you used the correct DuckID, which is the first part of your UO email address. It is not your 95# (UOID). If you used the correct DuckID, then contact caregivers@uoregon.edu for assistance. Do you already have a Care.com account? Apply your University of Oregon benefits to your existing Care.com account by visiting the uoregon.care.com portal and entering your email address registered with Care.com. You will be prompted to connect your account to the UO offering. Subscription costs associated with your previous account will stop automatically. Support and Resources Care.com is your go-to resource for assistance with account registration, site navigation, and any other questions you may have about your membership and their services. Accommodations will be provided to any individual who is unable to fully interact with the Care.com website. To request accommodations to use Care.com, email workplaceADA@uoregon.edu . Care Talks Care.com hosts a series of events and webinars to provide support and education on a variety of family care topics. Care Talks gives you access to advice and guidance from expert speakers in areas such as childcare, senior care, financial literacy, emotional health, and more. Care.com runs talks regularly on topics such as: Confident Parenting Raising Children with Disabilities Caring for Aging Loved Ones Financial Wellness Your Healthy Lifestyle Emotional Wellness Succeed at Work View Care Talks Schedule Employee Eligibility Employee groups - Faculty, officers of administration, classified employees, graduate employees.
